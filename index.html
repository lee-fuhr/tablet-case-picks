How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time how fast a hinge opens. Instead, we dig into what actually holds up, long-term owner feedback from forums, Reddit, and Amazon reviews, plus honest assessments from established tech reviewers. We look for cases that survive real-world abuse: keyboards that stay responsive after a year, covers that don’t peel, and magnets that keep your tablet secure. Our picks prioritize reliability over hype. We track common failure points like hinge wear, trackpad drift, and separation of the keyboard from the case. If a product has a pattern of breaking after a few months, it doesn’t make our list. We’re upfront about trade-offs, you won’t find a perfect case, but you will find one that fits your needs and lasts.
